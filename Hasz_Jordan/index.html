Why ORU?

I am studying at ORU because when I was looking for schools and visiting colleges, my parents suggested ORU. I attended a Quest Day and fell in love with the campus and atmosphere.

The things I liked most about attending ORU are:

  1. The spiritual atmosphere
  2. The friendships that I had made
  3. The cooler climate than back home
  4. The smaller campus
  5. The culture and feel of Tulsa


I decided to pursue a CIT degree because I want to go into cybersecurity. My parents and I felt that this degree would be the best path to get me there. To further support my goal, I am also minoring in criminal justice.
